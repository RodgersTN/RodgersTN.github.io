Research Interest

My current research interest focuses on exploring approaches to establish manually curated data resources for investigating the microbial degradation of pollutants in wastewater. Particularly exploring pollutant degrading microorganisms, catabolic genes, pollutant degrading enzymes, biodegradation pathways, degradation intermediates, and metabolic end products identified and their applications in microbial-mediated bioremediation strategies in environmental protection research studies.

Please refer to the Research section for more details about my research!
